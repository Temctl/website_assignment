Summary about our company

Our company was first established in the UK in 2003. It started with few friends who are really into cars. Which cuickly turned into big team that is now trying to find and collect all the vintage cars and get them into shape.

What do we do?

We collect vintage cars in hopes of preserving them and taking care of them. Once collected we will try to preserve any technology as is but if it needs repair we will do repairing to make it functional.
